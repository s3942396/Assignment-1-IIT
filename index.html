Project Idea

Overview

The project I have chosen to undertake is to make a game. I plan on making it a basic horror game, with a bit of a spin on the concept. The horror game will take place in the wild west era of America in which you play as a cowboy who has been stranded in an empty town with a killer on the loose. Exploring the town will reveal clues on its history and lead the cowboy to meeting the killer. Overall the game will be a standard horror game with an interesting setting which has not been overdone like other settings.

Motivation

The motivation behind me making a horror game would be it would help develop my skills in coding and game developing which is something I have been interested in for a while. Though there are millions upon millions of horror games available, I would still like to make a horror game as I think it would provide some levels of difficulty to make whilst not being the most difficult game I could try and develop. My main reason for the game would be for entertainment for others in addition to me wanting to produce a game and gain game development skills.

Description

The game would be a first person perspective horror game set in the wild west. A mysterious entity has wiped out a town of people. The protagonist is a cowboy and has arrived in the town to spend the night to then set out again. The cowboy will arrive in the town, hitch his horse outside the main saloon and heads on inside. No one is inside the bar, just empty seats and alcohol, so he begins to pour himself drinks. The cowboy downs a few drinks before being startled by his horse crying out for help. The cowboy runs out to his horse, to find it in a pool of blood and its head lopped off. From there the player is able to explore the town in search of clues for what has killed his horse and stranded him in this town. Throughout exploring the cowboy will encounter clues such as notes left behind by victims, body parts, blood trails and the likes. Various places the cowboy will be forced to explore will include a mine, houses, saloon, shops, a desert and a farm area. The narrative will follow a set path, and to prevent that player from going down the wrong path, keys and locked doors will be used. Over the course of the game the player must confront a mysterious entity that has been alluded to from the clues present in the game. The entity will later be found out to be a man enacting his revenge upon the town due to him being exiled prior. The man utilzes a sharpened shovel to murder people in the town, though to the player it will seem he is using some sort magic. During the game the cowboy will encounter survivors who have bunkered themselves within their respective areas. The cowboy will receive valuable information from them which will lead to the finale of the game, in which the player may choose to fight the man or leave unscathed. I will attempt to stylize the game to look old (this may change later) and will feature cut scenes, start menu screen and possibly loading screens. The UI of the game will be limited to mainly an inventory screen and a pause menu, as the game at this stage will not include crafting and the like. An example of a jumpscare that I will include will be when the player enters the abandoned mine and bats come flying out at the player. The mine will be the final area where the killer will be. The game will include eerie music, wild western music made to be scary and in general wildlife/scenery sounds. The game will be set entirely in the night time, and the town will be entirely lit up by small lanterns providing limited light. The cowboy will be forced to carry around his own light source in certain situations. The game will be made to utilise keyboard and mouse inputs, and settings for sensitivity, brightness and audio will be included in the pause menu.

Tools and Technologies

To make the game I will most likely be using the hardware of my PC which can run the Unity engine. The Unity engine will be where I make the game. I will most likely be using pre-made assets from Unity in the game but otherwise I will make my own assets via blender or the likes. Audio for the game I will attempt to make on my own, if not I will use audio from Unity or copyright free sounds. Photoshop will be used to create the UI, pause menu and start screen.

Skills Required

Skills that are required to make this game will be learning basic C++/ C# coding, understanding of Unity engine, understanding of blender and understanding of photoshop. These skills may be difficult to get but through use of tutorials on Youtube, and other websites I should be able to get a basic understanding of how to create the game. The Unity engine comes with built in tutorials to assist me as well. Coding in C++/C# will be difficult to learn but again tutorials via Youtube will help me program the game, same goes for Blender and making assets for the game, tutorials will be my saving grace.
